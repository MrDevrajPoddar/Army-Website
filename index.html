Theater of Operations

OPERATION SINDOOR

Operation Sindoor (May 2025)

Objective: Retaliatory Strike

Launched in response to the Pahalgam attack. Indian Armed Forces struck 9 terror camps in PoK targeting JeM, LeT, and Hizbul Mujahideen.

OPERATION MAHADEV

Operation Mahadev 2025

Objective: Counter-Terrorism

Joint operation by Indian Army, CRPF, and J&K Police to neutralize threats in the valley.

OPERATION BRAHMA

Operation Brahma (April 2025)

Objective: HADR Mission

Humanitarian aid to Myanmar. IAF and INS Gharial delivered food and emergency supplies to Yangon.

OPERATION SINDHU

Operation Sindhu 2025

Objective: Evacuation

Strategic evacuation of Indian citizens from Iran amid the ongoing conflict.

About Indian Army

The Indian Army is the land-based branch and the largest component of the Indian Armed Forces. The President of India is the Supreme Commander of the Indian Army, and its professional head is the Chief of Army Staff (COAS), who is a four-star general.
